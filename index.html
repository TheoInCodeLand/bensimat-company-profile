about Business



In the early stages of 2019, Bensimat Stocks emerged as a beacon of hope and opportunity. We proudly stand as a wholly black-owned establishment, strategically positioned to respond to the ever-evolving economic landscape and the increasing demand for a wide array of essential items.

Our journey began with a deep understanding of the financial challenges faced by many who aspire to access certified, high-quality working equipment. It is this understanding that fuels our unwavering mission â€“ to make top-tier equipment accessible to all, offering not just products but empowerment at affordable prices.

Bensimat Stocks carries the distinguished honor of being a Level 1 B-BBEE Contributor, a testament to our unswerving commitment to transformation. As a 100% black-owned private company, we exemplify the principles of equity and inclusion. Our procurement and employment policies are designed with a conscious bias toward previously disadvantaged groups, thereby fortifying our dedication to creating opportunities that empower. Within this, we place a special emphasis on supporting enterprises owned by youth and black women, recognizing their role as catalysts for societal change.

At Bensimat Stocks, we are more than just a business; we are a force for economic and social advancement. Join us on this transformative journey as we set new standards, reshape industries, and stand as a paragon of professionalism and inclusivity.
